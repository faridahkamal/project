Crab and Avocado Salad

Category:Healthy

How to make Crab and Avocado Salad, Jan 21, 2022

Faridah's Recipe Book

  • Prep: 25min
  • Cook: 5min
  • Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  1. Kosher salt
  2. 1/2 pound haricots verts or green beans, halved
  3. 1/3 cup whole wheat flour
  4. 2/3 cup low-fat plain yogurt
  5. 3 tablespoons low-fat mayonnaise
  6. 1 to 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  7. 1/2 cup chopped fresh chives
  8. 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  9. 3 anchovy fillets, chopped
  10. Freshly ground pepper
  11. 1/2 pound lump or claw crabmeat
  12. 1 Hass avocado, halved, pitted and diced
  13. 3 romaine hearts, chopped
  14. 1 1/2 cups whole-wheat croutons
  15. 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved

Directions:

Step 1:Bring a saucepan of salted water to a boil. Add the haricots verts and cook until crisp-tender, 3 to 5 minutes. Drain and run under cold water to stop the cooking.

Step 2:Puree the yogurt, mayonnaise, lemon juice, chives, basil and anchovies in a blender until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 3:Toss the crabmeat, half of the avocado and about 1 tablespoon of the yogurt dressing in a small bowl. Toss the romaine, croutons, haricots verts and the remaining avocado with the remaining dressing in a large bowl

Step 4:Divide the romaine salad among plates, top with the tomatoes and place some of the crab mixture in the center.
