Roasted Carrots

Category:Medicinal

How to make Roasted Carrots, Jan 21, 2022

Faridah's Recipe Book

  • Time: 30min
  • Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  1. 12 carrots
  2. 3 tablespoons good olive oil
  3. 1 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt
  4. 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
  5. 2 tablespoons minced fresh dill or parsley

Directions:

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.

Step 2:If the carrots are thick, cut them in half lengthwise; if not, leave whole. Slice the carrots diagonally in 1 1/2-inch-thick slices. (The carrots will shrink while cooking so make the slices big.) Toss them in a bowl with the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Transfer to a sheet pan in 1 layer and roast in the oven for 20 minutes, until browned and tender.

Step 3:Toss the carrots with minced dill or parsley, season to taste, and serve.
